🏢 About Stackly

We're building the future
of financial technology

Stackly was born from a simple idea: make financial intelligence accessible, powerful, and beautifully simple. Today, we're proud to serve thousands of businesses across the globe.

✦ Our Mission

Empowering the next generation
of financial innovation

We believe that financial technology should be open, intelligent, and human‑centered. Our mission is to democratize access to world‑class financial tools.

Stackly was founded in 2020 by a team of engineers, designers, and finance professionals who saw an opportunity to rebuild financial infrastructure from the ground up. We combine cutting‑edge AI with a relentless focus on user experience.

Today, we serve over 18,000 active users across 54 countries, processing millions of transactions every month. Our platform is trusted by startups, enterprises, and everyone in between.
